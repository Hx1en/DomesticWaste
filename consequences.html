What are the consequences of domestic waste?

  • Human health impact:: If not managed properly, it cause people to suffer from illnesses such as eye difficulties, respiratory problems, gastric problems, skin problems and for animals it can cause many diseases and even death.
  • Adverse effects on animals:Animals such as cows and buffalos ingest plastics together with their food, resulting in the death of the animals. This has an impact on the quality and quantity of animal milk products.
  • Land pollution: Drain obstructions occur as a result of abandoned plastic carry bags and uncollected debris, resulting in stagnant water that serves as a breeding ground for mosquitoes and insects.
