What does ‘reduce’ mean in everyday use?

In everyday use, reduce means to make something smaller, fewer, or less in amount, size, degree, or price. It is commonly used to mean cutting down on consumption or waste to minimize environmental impact.

How can you reduce household waste?

Making one small change can make a significant difference to the environment and save you money. Here are some suggestions:

  • Avoid single-use items, e.g. razors, batteries, wipes, plastic bags
  • Make a shopping list to reduce food waste
  • Switch to rechargeable batteries
  • Check expire dates to avoid buying food that might be thrown out if not eaten immediately
  • Buy more environmentally friendly products
  • Purchase items with less packaging
